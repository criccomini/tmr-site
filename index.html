Authors

Chris Riccomini is a software engineer with more than a decade of experience at Silicon Valley tech companies including PayPal, LinkedIn, and WePay, a JP Morgan Chase Company. Over his career, Chris has held titles as data scientist, staff software engineer, and distinguished software engineer. He has managed engineering teams in the distributed systems and payments space.

Dmitriy Ryaboy has been a software engineer and manager since the turn of the century. He holds an EECS degree from UC Berkeley and a Masters in "Very Large Information Systems", which is what they called “Big Data” back in the day, from CMU. He’s worked at places ranging from large government institutions (Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory), to tiny startups (Cloudera, when it was 12 people), to small but rapidly growing and maturing internet startups (Twitter). He is currently the Vice President of software engineering at Zymergen, a company that combines machine learning, robotics, and cutting edge bioengineering to create new materials for the 21st century.
